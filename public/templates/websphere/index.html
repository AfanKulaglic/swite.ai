FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our hosting services

Q: What is included in the hosting plans?

All our hosting plans include free SSL certificates, daily automated backups, 24/7 expert support, 99.9% uptime guarantee, and one-click WordPress installation. You also get access to our intuitive control panel, email hosting, and unlimited bandwidth.

Q: Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time with just a few clicks. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the cost difference. There are no penalties or hidden fees for changing plans.

Q: Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied with our service for any reason, contact us within 30 days for a full refund. No questions asked, no hassle.

Q: How do I migrate my existing website?

We offer free website migration for all new customers. Our expert team will handle the entire migration process for you, ensuring zero downtime and data integrity. Just contact our support team with your current hosting details to get started.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway with PCI DSS compliance.

Q: Is my data backed up automatically?

Yes, we perform daily automated backups of all your data. Business and Enterprise plans include more frequent backups. You can restore your website to any previous backup point with one click from your control panel.

Q: What kind of support do you provide?

We provide 24/7 support through live chat, email tickets, and phone. Our expert support team has an average response time of under 2 minutes for live chat and under 1 hour for email tickets. We also have extensive documentation and video tutorials.

Q: Can I host multiple websites on one plan?

The Starter plan supports 1 website, while Business and Enterprise plans support unlimited websites. You can host as many domains as you need on a single account with our higher-tier plans.
